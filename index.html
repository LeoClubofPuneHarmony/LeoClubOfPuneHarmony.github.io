Welcome to Leo Club Of Pune Harmony

Building stronger communities through service, leadership, and friendship.

Leo Club of Pune Harmony is a youth-led service group dedicated to empowering young people, supporting local causes, and making a positive impact in Pune and beyond.

Join our mission

Who We Are

We are a community of students and young professionals who come together to serve others, grow as leaders, and create meaningful friendships. Our club organizes social events, outreach programs, educational initiatives, and charity activities across Pune.

Community Service

We organize regular drives, cleanups, and outreach events that help vulnerable families, children, and seniors in our city.

Youth Leadership

Members receive leadership training, public speaking practice, and project planning experience to become stronger community leaders.

Friendship and Growth

Our club provides a warm and welcoming environment where members connect, learn, and support each other while making a difference.

Our Current Focus

We are devoted to uplifting local schools, supporting health awareness campaigns, and organizing activities that celebrate culture, harmony, and social responsibility.

  • Support education and literacy projects.
  • Promote health and wellness awareness.
  • Build sustainable community partnerships.

Whether you want to volunteer, lead a project, or collaborate with us, our club is open to anyone who wants to contribute with heart and purpose.

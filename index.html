Welcome to Leo Club Of Pune Harmony

Building stronger communities through service, leadership, and friendship.

Leo Club of Pune Harmony is a youth-led service group dedicated to empowering young people, supporting local causes, and making a positive impact in Pune and beyond.

Join our mission

Who We Are

We are a community of students and young professionals who come together to serve others, grow as leaders, and create meaningful friendships. Our club organizes social events, outreach programs, educational initiatives, and charity activities across Pune.

Community Service

We organize regular drives, cleanups, and outreach events that help vulnerable families, children, and seniors in our city.

Youth Leadership

Members receive leadership training, public speaking practice, and project planning experience to become stronger community leaders.

Friendship and Growth

Our club provides a warm and welcoming environment where members connect, learn, and support each other while making a difference.

Our Current Focus

We are devoted to uplifting local schools, supporting health awareness campaigns, and organizing activities that celebrate culture, harmony, and social responsibility.

  • Support education and literacy projects.
  • Promote health and wellness awareness.
  • Build sustainable community partnerships.

Roar! I'm Leo — Our 12-Month Roadmap

I lead the pride in serving Pune. Each month I roar about a new initiative — here’s what we proudly do.

01 | Old Age Home Visits

Organizing uplifting community events and recreational games to interact with senior citizens in local old-age homes, offering companionship, warmth, and shared joy.

02 | Rebuilding Learning Spaces

Conducting interactive workshops that empower students to actively optimize, clean, and re-engineer their environments into healthy, vibrant, and sustainable places for education.

03 | Book Recycling & Sharing Network

Driving continuous regional book collection campaigns to harvest unused textbooks, notebooks, and educational supplies, distributing them straight to underprivileged students.

04 | Hair Donation for Cancer Patients

Spreading clinical public awareness regarding wig requirements for cancer patients undergoing treatment, while organizing and coordinating healthy hair donation drives.

05 | Pune Awareness Sign Initiative

Creating custom, structural environmental sign installations and messaging indicators across strategic urban areas to build public awareness on localized sustainability and civic issues.

06 | Healthy Homemaker Kitchen Network

Connecting passionate local homemakers directly with customer markets to provide fresh, wholesome homemade meals—scaling viable micro-entrepreneurship channels for women.

07 | Rain Readiness Stations

Deploying dedicated seasonal assistance hubs in busy public areas carrying emergency umbrellas, protective raincoats, fresh drinking water, first-aid tools, and phone charging points.

08 | Local Heroes – Talent Discovery Program

Discovering gifted youth and parents from fragile or underprivileged locations, documenting their inspiring stories, and connecting them to educational scholarships and mentorship networks.

09 | HerSpark

Mentoring female founders through strategic business design workshops, tactical idea-structuring setups, and practical assistance in scaling initial concepts into active community ventures.

10 | Lost & Found to Loved & Used

Systematically collecting, sanitizing, sorting, and redistributing wearable clothing, bags, stationery, and toys to needy families, minimizing the local waste footprint.

11 | Beat the Heat

An applied engineering workshop teaching students scientific first-principles to design and build working cooling units out of basic, everyday recycled resources.

12 | Access Bridge

Hosting localized digital training and structural aid desks helping underprivileged individuals fill out critical state forms, navigate modern application setups, and cross the digital divide.

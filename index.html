Leo Club Of Pune Harmony

A dynamic, youth-led community service network focused on uplifting individuals, engineering solutions, and inspiring continuous communal service across Pune.

Annual Action Initiatives

Hover over our calendar roadmap cards to interact with our impact journey

Initiative 01

Old Age Home Visits

Organizing uplifting interactions and recreational games to connect with senior citizens, offering companionship and joy.

Initiative 02

Rebuilding Learning Spaces

Conducting workshops to help students optimize, clean, and re-engineer their environments into healthy and sustainable places for education.

Initiative 03

Book Recycling & Sharing Network

Driving continuous collection campaigns to recycle unused books and supplies, distributing them straight to underprivileged students.

Initiative 04

Hair Donation for Cancer Patients

Spreading public awareness regarding clinical wig requirements for cancer patients, coordinating healthy hair collections.

Initiative 05

Pune Awareness Sign Initiative

Creating custom environmental sign installations and messaging cards around the city to build structural public awareness on sustainability issues.

Initiative 06

Healthy Homemaker Kitchen Network

Connecting passionate homemakers directly with local food markets to provide homemade meals, scaling micro-entrepreneurship channels for women.

Initiative 07

Rain Readiness Stations

Deploying specialized seasonal assistance hubs carrying umbrellas, protective raincoats, drinking water, first-aid tools, and emergency charging systems.

Initiative 08

Local Heroes – Talent Discovery Program

Discovering gifted youth and community figures from fragile locations, creating public spotlights, and connecting them to educational scholarships.

Initiative 09

HerSpark

Mentoring female founders through strategic business workshops, idea structuring setups, and practical assistance in scaling concepts to ventures.

Initiative 10

Lost & Found to Loved & Used

Systematically collecting, sanitizing, and sorting wearable clothing, bags, and toys to distribute them to communities while reducing waste footprints.

Initiative 11

Beat the Heat

An applied engineering workshop teaching students scientific first-principles to craft working cooling units out of basic everyday resources.

Initiative 12

Access Bridge

Hosting localized training support helping individuals fill out critical state forms, applications, and navigate modern digital layouts.
